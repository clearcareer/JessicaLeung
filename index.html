About Jess

Jess Leung is a Chinese-Canadian singer-songwriter from Richmond Hill, Ontario. Though a classical flutist and teacher by trade, Jess’ greatest passion lies in her singing and songwriting. Inspired by nature’s beauty and life’s intimate moments, Jessica shares personal reflections with her sweet and silvery voice. Influenced by artists including Ingrid Michaelson, Ellie Goulding, Coldplay, Stromae, Oh Wonder, and Tori Kelly, Jess’ jazz indie pop sound combines her early indie folk background with her current exploration of jazz, pop, and R&B. Currently writing and recording her original music, Jess is set to release her first EP in late 2017.
