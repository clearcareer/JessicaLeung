About Jess

Jessica Leung, born and raised in Richmond Hill, Ontario, is an up and coming singer-songwriter in the Toronto scene. Her influences include Tori Kelly, Stromae, Ingrid Michaelson, Regina Spektor, Ellie Goulding, and Oh Wonder. Jessica's songwriting is uniquely musical and imaginative, with a pure and sweet, yet warm and powerful voice. After graduating from the University of Toronto majoring in classical flute, Jessica has been focusing on her singing career in the last year, performing at the Mod Club, Revival Club, and with the Raptors 905 team and Art of Time Ensemble. Jessica is the 2016 winner of the Landmark Events Showcase Festival in Toronto, a top 10 Regional Finalist of the 2016 CBC Music Searchlight, and most recently performed at Canadian Music Week. Jessica is now working on her EP, set to be released in early 2017.
